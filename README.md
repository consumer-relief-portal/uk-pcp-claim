# UK PCP Claim - Motor Finance Redress Portal

Welcome to the UK PCP Claim portal. This resource provides comprehensive information and support for consumers seeking compensation for mis-sold personal contract purchase (PCP) and hire purchase (HP) agreements.

## Overview

The UK PCP Claim portal is designed to assist consumers in:
- Understanding discretionary commission arrangements (DCA)
- Determining eligibility for compensation claims
- Accessing professional legal assessment and support
- Filing formal claims with lenders and regulatory bodies
- Navigating the motor finance redress process

## Features

### Core Resources
- **Eligibility Assessment** - Check if your agreement qualifies for redress
- **Claim Calculator** - Estimate potential compensation amounts
- **Legal Support Directory** - Access to qualified legal coordinators
- **Documentation Guides** - Step-by-step filing assistance
- **FAQ Section** - Answers to common motor finance questions
- **Regional Support** - Local specialist teams across the UK

### Supported Topics
- Personal Contract Purchase (PCP) agreements (2007-2021)
- Hire Purchase (HP) agreements with DCA
- Discretionary Commission Arrangements (DCA) audits
- Interest rate manipulation detection
- Settlement negotiation strategies
- Regulatory body contact information

- Unfair Terms in Consumer Contracts Regulations

## Avoiding Scams

**Red flags to watch for:**
- Upfront payment demands before claim filing
- Guaranteed compensation promises
- Pressure to act immediately
- Claims of exclusive or secret programs
- Unverified or unregistered legal services

## Finding Support

**Free Resources:**
- Citizens Advice Bureau (CAB)
- Consumer Rights Center
- Financial Ombudsman Service
- Solicitors Regulation Authority (find qualified solicitors)
- Trading Standards offices

**How to File Directly:**
- Contact your vehicle finance lender directly
- Submit statutory Subject Access Request (SAR)
- File complaint with Financial Ombudsman Service
- Consult qualified solicitors or legal advisors
